Love to hate Sonnet

We love to hate

We hate to love

Smoking sucks

So rise above

School is boring

And full of work

But worse of all

is the homework

And sounds too

Like slurping loudly

And breathing warm air

Something I do hardly

We love to hate

We hate to love
